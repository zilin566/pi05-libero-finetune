## Successful rollouts

### `success_task1_two_objects_to_basket.mp4`

- Task: put both the cream cheese box and the butter in the basket
- Result: success
- Purpose: demonstrates sequential two-object manipulation.

### `success_task4_two_mugs_two_plates.mp4`

- Task: put the white mug on the left plate and the yellow-white mug on the right plate
- Result: success
- Purpose: demonstrates object-target association and multi-stage placement.

### `success_task5_book_back_compartment.mp4`

- Task: pick up the book and place it in the back compartment of the caddy
- Result: success
- Purpose: successful comparison for the boundary-sensitive placement task.

## Failed rollouts

### `failure_task5_place_outside_target.mp4`

- Task: book placement in the back compartment
- Result: failure
- Primary label: `place_outside_target`
- Secondary diagnosis: `x_axis_containment_miss`

### `failure_task8_second_object_grasp.mp4`

- Task: put both moka pots on the stove
- Result: failure
- Primary label: `timeout_or_oscillation`
- Secondary diagnosis: `second_object_grasp_failure`

### `failure_task9_microwave_placement.mp4`

- Task: put the yellow-white mug in the microwave and close it
- Result: failure
- Primary label: `place_outside_target`
- Secondary diagnosis: `premature_release_at_microwave_threshold`
